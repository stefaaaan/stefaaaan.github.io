Small and cozy place

Reviewed 2 days ago

9.0

During my business trip in Bucharest, I had free time to explore the city and tried to look for a place bit out of touristic ones in old town. Found this one within walkable distance from old town, even if it’s bit hidden (google map helped :) ). Place is not that big but cozy. Meals are like at home, so if you are looking for fancy ones, maybe it’s not for you. But food was so tasty, very good portion and so cheap! For half liter beer, potatoes with rosemary and mici (grilled minced meat) i paid less than 6 eur! Service was good, despite many people eating at that moment. Highly recommended! :)

Thrilled by the good reviews me and my wife decided to eat here as it’s really close to our house. We ordered the mutton pastrami with polenta. When the order came after the first bite we both had the same reaction:”this is not mutton”...we called for the waiter and when we asked him if it is really mutton he assured us that the cook does a special seasoning and the mutton doesn’t taste as mutton blablabala. Hilarious. It was really pork. So first and last visit for us. To be honest i feel scammed as it never happened to me before. Well I guess it’s a first for everything... Would definitely not recommended this.
